Product Feature Guide (Developer Specification)
System Overview
The Concertina HR Team TimeTracker & Time Away Management System is an internal platform designed to manage Independent Contractor attendance, time tracking, leave requests, and internal communications.
The system will support three levels of access:

	•	Team Member Access – Regular Independent Contractors
	•	Supervisor Access – Team leads / managers
	•	Super Admin Access – HR / system administrators 
Each access level has different permissions and system visibility.
